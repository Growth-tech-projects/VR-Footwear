Our Story

Community Trusted
Since 2011

V. R. Footwear is a neighborhood shoe retailer located in Banashankari 3rd Stage, Bengaluru. Established in 2011, we serve as a one-stop destination for affordable and branded footwear, including selections from well-known Indian brands like Lancer and Liberty.

Known for courteous service and value pricing, our dedicated staff is always ready to assist you with fitting, product selection, and after-sales queries. Located conveniently in a mixed residential-commercial area accessible by BMTC buses and auto-rickshaws, we've grown alongside our community to match evolving urban footwear trends.
